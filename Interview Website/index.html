A Magical Interview With Myself

Wow. What magic... Incredible.

Why do you want to be a Designer?—

Well. When I was a young boy my Mother took me into the city to see a Tech Convention.
She said, "Son, when you grow up will you be the saviour of the broken, incoherent, and the damned?"
"Will you design it? Website and the graphics....with the plans clients have made?"

What worries you about being a designer?—

That my Mom was actually wrong and I will not be the saviour.

What excites you about it?—

I am an ADHD super-brain, if I am not excited you're probably in trouble.
So, basically everything.

What aspect of design do you gravitate towards the most?—

Absolutely, without a doubt it has to be meme culture.

What role or responsibility does a designer play in our society?—

To accurately and effectively communicate ideas and information.

What is a website?—

A series of letters, numbers, and images thrust through a series of tubes and most commonly viewed from your hand.
